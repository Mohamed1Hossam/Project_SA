Now in its sophomore year, the seventh-generation 2025 Mustang continues the course it charted when it was born back in the 1960s: deliver a thoroughly American take on the sporty coupe experience. Variety remains a key component of its success, with the 2025 ‘Stang available in varying levels of intensity from a 315-horsepower turbo four-cylinder to a 480-hp 5.0-liter V-8— not to mention the edgier 500-hp Mustang Dark Horse. The latter two can be optioned with a six-speed manual transmission to replace the stock 10-speed automatic, but the turbo four is an automatic-only affair.
